What is the perimeter of the polygon?
The perimeter of a polygon is the total amount of distance around the polygon.
How to calculate perimeter of the polygon?
To calculate perimeter of the polygon you should use the following formula:
perimeter of the polygon = number of sides of polygon * length of any side of polygon
